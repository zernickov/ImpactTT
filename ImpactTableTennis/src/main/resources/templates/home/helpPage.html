How To Find A Match?

To start af match you need to click the "Find Match" button located in the column called "Quick Match" right under your profile information

The "Find Match" button looks like this:

After this your profile will be transfered to the second column: "Next Match", where you will stay until the system has found an opponent for you

Note that if the opponent was found before or at the same time that you searched for a match you will be directly transfered to the "Upcoming Matches" overview

"Upcoming Matches" represents the queue of unfinished matched

After you have finished your match you need to declare whether you've lost or won the match

This is done by pressing the appropriate button, the two buttons look like this:

The match has now been stored in the system and your ELO has been updated

ELO DESCRIPTION: The ELO-rating system is a method of calculating the relative skill levels of players in zero-sum games, meaning the higher the ELO the better the player
